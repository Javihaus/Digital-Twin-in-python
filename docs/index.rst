otwin documentation
===================

**otwin** — physics-informed digital twins with calibrated uncertainty and
leakage-free evaluation by default. Lightweight, CPU-first.

One pattern across a spectrum of physics: a structured physical prior
(port-Hamiltonian at the strong end, an empirical law at the light end), a
learned residual, calibrated uncertainty, and evaluation without leakage.
